Lasagna

Some of the best pasta out there, second only to ravioli

A favorite of the popular cat Garfield, you can't go wrong with a delicious plage of warm lasagna

Ingredients
  • 1 lb sausage
  • 3/4 lb ground beef
  • 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 can tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz recotta cheese

Steps
  1. Cook meat in an oven
  2. Boil noodles in water and mix cheese
  3. Preheat oven to 375
  4. Spread meat and cheese over noddles and make layers
  5. Bake in over for 25 minutes and let cool for 15
